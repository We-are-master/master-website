  • Relevant experienceA track record in your trade and the type of work you take on.
  • Minimum £1M Public Liability InsuranceValid cover for the work you carry out on client sites.
  • Relevant certificationsGas Safe, NICEIC, NAPIT, OFTEC or equivalent where required.

Do I own the site?
On the one-time plan, yes, outright. On the monthly plan, your site, hosting and tools run for as long as you're subscribed.
What if I cancel the monthly plan?
Cancel anytime, no contract and no fee. There's nothing to lose by trying it.
Why do I pay before the onboarding call?
It lets us start immediately and reserve your build slot, instead of chasing payment for a week. It's fully refundable before work begins, so it's risk-free and just gets you live faster.
What do I need to provide?
Your logo (if you have one), 5–10 photos of your work, your list of services and typical pricing, and your hours and service area. We guide you through all of it on the call.
How is it really live in 7 days?
A proven process plus your onboarding answers. Because the quiz and call capture exactly what we need up front, there's no back-and-forth, we build, you review, you go live.
Does it work for my trade?
If you do home service work, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, roofing, landscaping, cleaning, remodeling, handyman and more, the booking system is built around how you quote and schedule.
What happens to bookings?
Every booking and lead drops straight into your CRM, tagged and ready, with automated replies, reminders and follow-ups so nothing slips.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can switch between one-time and monthly at checkout, and we'll help you move later if your needs change.
